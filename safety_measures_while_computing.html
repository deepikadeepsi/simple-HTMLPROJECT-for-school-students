SAFETY MEASURES WHILE USING COMPUTER OR INTERNET.

We can take the following safety measures while using Internet or computer:

  1. Install updated antivirus in your computer system.
  2. Be cautious while using your password online.
  3. Don't entertain strangers or suspicious mails.
  4. Don't share your personal details with anyone online.
  5. Avoid friend requests from strangers.
    6. Keep your privacy settings always on.
  6. Be careful what you download.
    7. Think before you post anything on social networking sites.
  7. To maintain data privacy while transferring file from one place to another, Encryption can be implemented.
